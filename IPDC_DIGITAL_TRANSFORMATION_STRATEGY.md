# 🏦 IPDC CRM Digital Transformation Strategy & TAT Analysis

## 1. Digital Transformation Blueprint
This blueprint outlines the roadmap for migrating IPDC’s CRM department from a manual-heavy workflow to an AI-augmented "Intelligent CRM."

### A. Data Integration (The RAG Foundation)
*   Centralize 100% of internal credit manuals and Bangladesh Bank circulars.
*   Implement Semantic Indexing (Vectorization) to allow for "Natural Language" queries instead of keyword searches.

### B. Workflow Orchestration (The Automation Layer)
*   **Real-time Updating:** Connecting to regulatory feeds via webhooks (e.g., n8n).
*   **Zero-Entry Checkers:** Automatically extracting data from client NID and Bank Statements into the AI Assistant.

## 3. Human-AI Augmentation Strategy
The goal of this research is not to replace the CRM Officer, but to **Augment** them. 

### "The Co-Pilot Workflow"
1.  **AI Path (Clerical):** The AI Agent performs the "Low-Level" cognitive tasks: Reading documents, checking cross-defaults, and performing LTV/DBR math.
2.  **Human Path (Expert):** The CRM Officer focuses on "High-Level" judgment: Analyzing the client’s industry stability, relationship history, and ethical risk profile.
3.  **Synthesis:** The AI provides a "Decision Support" report, and the Human makes the "Final Dissection" and recommendation.

### **Conclusion**
By offloading **Regulatory Search** and **Mathematical Verification** to the AI, the IPDC CRM team can handle a **higher volume of loan applications** without increasing headcount, ensuring the bank's growth is supported by efficient technology.
