# Thesis Proposal 3: Financial Inclusion & Alternative Data

**Title:** Expanding the Portfolio: Assessing the Benefits of AI-Driven Alternative Data Analysis for SME Credit Growth at IPDC Finance  
**Department:** CRM, IPDC Finance Ltd.  

## 1. Introduction
SMEs are the backbone of the Bangladesh economy, but many remain "unbanked" or "credit invisible" because they do not have the formal paperwork (audited financials, tax returns) that IPDC’s CRM traditionally requires. AI offers a way to bridge this gap using non-traditional data.

## 2. Problem Statement
The current CRM framework at IPDC is designed for "Data-Rich" corporate clients. When applied to "Data-Poor" SMEs, it leads to high rejection rates or high collateral requirements. This limits IPDC’s market growth. AI technologies (like Machine Learning) can extract creditworthiness signals from data such as utility bills, mobile money (bKash/Nagad) trends, and supply chain records.

## 3. Research Aim & Objectives
The aim is to design and assess an AI-driven "Alternative Credit Scoring" (ACS) model for SME lending.
1. To identify the most predictive "Alternative Data" sources available in the Bangladesh market.
2. To develop a prototype ML model that uses this data to predict repayment probability.
3. To simulate the potential growth in IPDC’s SME portfolio if this AI model were adopted.
4. To analyze the risk-adjusted profitability of "Alternative Data" borrowers compared to traditional borrowers.

## 4. Research Questions
1. Which non-traditional data sources are the most reliable indicators of creditworthiness for Bangladeshi SMEs?
2. How does an AI-based alternative scoring model compare in accuracy to traditional "Paper-Based" assessment?
3. What is the potential impact on IPDC's market share in the SME segment?

## 5. Methodology
**Quantitative Approach:**
*   **Predictive Modeling:** Using Machine Learning (XGBoost or Random Forest) on a sample of SME data.
*   **Simulation:** Applying the model to previously "Rejected" applications to see how many would be "Safely Accepted" using alternative data.
